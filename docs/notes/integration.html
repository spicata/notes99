integration

\(\int ax^{n} \, dx =\frac{ax^{n+1}}{n+1}+c\) \(\int [f(x)]^{n}f'(x) \, dx =\frac{[f(x)]^{n+1}}{n+1}+c\) \(\int e^{f(x)}f'(x) \, dx =e^{f(x)}+c\) \(\int \sin[f(x)]f'(x) \, dx =-\cos[f(x)]+c\) \(\int \cos[f(x)]f'(x) \, dx =\sin[f(x)]+c\) \(\int \frac{f'(x)}{f(x)} \, dx =\ln|f(x)|+c\)

\(\int kf(x) \, dx =k\int f(x) \, dx\) \(\int f(x) \, dx =\frac{1}{k}\int kf(x) \, dx\)
