When one element both oxidises and reduces in an equation, that reaction is called disproportionation. i.e.

$$2H_{2}O_{2}\to 2H_{2}O + O_{2}$$

The opposite would be a comproportionation.

$$2H_{2}O + O_{2}\to 2H_{2}O_{2}$$
