The number plane (argand diagram) (complex plane). Like a Cartesian plane but for number line and complex.

All real numbers are just in the complex plane, if you think about it. Now we have: N > Z > Q > R > C. Real numbers are just sepcial complex numbers.

You can also think of them as vectors. Addition and subtraction does the same as vector addition.

You can also apply... transformations? If you apply any complex number by -1, you rotate it by 180deg. Rotate it by i to rotate it 90deg counterclockwise.
