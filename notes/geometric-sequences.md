Geometric sequences have a common ratio (r) and a starting term. In recursive terms:

$$T_{n}=T_{n-1}\times r$$

A general sequence:

$$a, ar, ar^{2}, ar^{3}, \dots$$

General term:

$$T_{n}=ar^{(n-1)}$$
