# GST
```ad-tldr

GST is goods and service tax. It is tax that is applied on things you buy.

```
Imagine a cookie that costs $80 dollars, with a 10% GST. How much is the cookie?
$$\begin{align}
GST&=P\cdot R\\
GST&=80\cdot10\% \\
GST&=80\cdot0.1 \\
GST&=8
\end{align}$$
